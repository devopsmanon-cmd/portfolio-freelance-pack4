Pack 4 — Full case study

Case study: Local salon (Montreuil) - 90 days

A realistic demonstration project: full redesign, local SEO, conversion and technical stability. The goal is to show method and outcomes, not vague claims.

Request a quoteSee services

Client context

Initial situation

  • Outdated and slow website, weak mobile clarity
  • No local SEO structure
  • Low contact conversion
  • Heavy reliance on word of mouth

Business goals

  • Increase qualified inquiries
  • Improve local visibility (Montreuil + nearby areas)
  • Build trust through premium positioning
  • Create a scalable base for SEO + Ads

Applied strategy

1) Architecture

Service pages + zone pages + consistent internal linking. Clean heading hierarchy and clear search intent per page.

2) Conversion

Rewritten key blocks, shorter path to action, trust elements and direct contact in top sections.

3) Technical

Resource optimization, on-page SEO hygiene, clean deployment and monitoring baseline.

Execution timeline (90 days)

Week 1-2

Audit, scope, page mapping, structural wireframe.

Week 3-6

Production, local SEO pages, conversion integration.

Week 7-12

Go-live, tuning via Search Console/Analytics, iteration on priority pages.

Observed results (90 days)

SEO & traffic

  • +138% organic impressions
  • +82% organic clicks
  • 12 local keywords in Top 10
Source: realistic simulation based on similar local projects.

Conversion

  • Conversion rate: 1.4% -> 3.2%
  • Monthly inquiries: x2.1
  • Avg. session duration: +44%
Combined effect: clarity + speed + CTA + trust.

Stack & deliverables

Deliverables

  • Multi-page, multi-zone structure
  • Reusable service-page template
  • Quarterly optimization roadmap
  • Operational handover notes

Out of scope

  • Google ad spend
  • Unlimited content production
  • 24/7 support

Note: this is a realistic demonstration case while named client case studies are being prepared.

What this proves

Performance is never one single trick. It is a system: SEO architecture, trust-oriented UX, clean technical execution and iterative improvements. That is the Pack 4 logic.
