Frequently asked questions

How long does delivery take?

Depends on the package and feedback speed. Pack 1 is quick, Pack 2/3 usually a few days to 2 weeks, Pack 4 depends on volume.

How does payment work?

40% deposit to start. Balance on delivery. Payment by bank transfer (recommended) or PayPal (fees paid by client).

Can I pay Google to rank #1?

You cannot pay for #1 organic SEO directly. You can improve SEO over time, and/or run Google Ads for immediate top ad visibility.

Is Google Ads included?

Ads management is an option. The Google ad spend itself is never included in management fees.

Do you handle hosting?

Depends on package/options: simple hosting or VPS setup. Clean deployment (HTTPS, Nginx, performance, monitoring) can be included.

Is maintenance mandatory?

No, but recommended for stability: uptime, alerts, updates and performance tracking.

Do you also write content?

Yes: structure + optimize + refine your copy. Pack 4 includes deeper content strategy.

How does multilingual Pack 4 work?

Each language has dedicated pages (FR/EN/DE), separate SEO structure and hreflang implementation.

What do you need from me?

Business name, city/area, services, hours, contacts, logo/photos, and 2-3 competitors to understand the market.
