Pricing & Payment

Package pricing and terms

Full transparency: clear packages, visible options and explicit payment conditions.

Payment terms

General rule

  • 40% deposit to start the project
  • 60% on delivery (go-live after final payment)
  • A written quote is provided before starting

Per package

  • Pack 1 — €1,000: 40% (€400) + 60% (€600)
  • Pack 2 — €1,300: 40% (€520) + 60% (€780)
  • Pack 3 — €2,200: 40% (€880) + 30% (€660) + 30% (€660)
  • Pack 4 — from €4,000: 40% + 20% + 20% + 20%

Pack 4 scope varies based on pages, languages, locations and content. The quote defines the final price.

Payment methods

  • Bank transfer (recommended)
  • PayPal (PayPal fees paid by the client)

Work starts once the deposit is received. Invoice provided (micro-business).
